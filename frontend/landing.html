Built for the student journey

From your first application to your first offer letter — CareerTrack has every step covered.

📋
Application Tracker
Track every internship and job application with status updates — Applied, Assessment, Interview, Selected, or Rejected. Search and filter instantly.
🎙
Interview Rounds
Log every interview round with type, date, outcome, prep status, and notes. Click the outcome badge to cycle through results instantly.
📄
PDF Resume Parser
Upload your resume on first login. The system automatically extracts your skills, certifications, and projects — no manual entry needed.
📊
Career Dashboard
See your success rate, monthly activity, and application breakdown through interactive charts powered by SQL aggregate queries.
💼
Internship Experience
Track actual internships — company, role, dates, stipend, skills used, and completion certificate. Mark Ongoing or Completed.
🗂
Resume Versions
Maintain multiple resume versions and see which one gets more responses. Link each application to the resume you actually submitted.
⚡
Skills & Certifications
Track skills grouped by proficiency and certifications with expiry warnings. Auto-imported from your resume or added manually.
🔐
Secure & Personal
JWT authentication and bcrypt password hashing keep your data private. Everything linked to your account only.
Simple flow

How it works

From signup to dashboard in minutes.

1
Register your account
Create an account with your name, USN, branch, semester and CGPA.
2
Upload your resume
Upload your resume PDF. The system extracts your skills and certifications automatically. Review and confirm in one click.
3
Start tracking applications
Add internship applications, update their status as things progress, and log every interview round with notes.
4
Get selected — track the internship
When you mark an application as Selected, the system asks if you want to add it to your Experience tracker. One click and it's done.
5
Watch your career grow
The dashboard shows your success rate, monthly activity, and skill growth over time.
